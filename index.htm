Breadth

This series showcases the scope, the breadth, of my artistic endeavors. It is a compilation of some of my general pieces- regardless of the inspiration or setting in which they were conceived.

The Breadth series of art spans a wide variety of subjects, media, and styles. I aspire to showcase but a humble sampling of my work throughout this category.




Concentration

Concentration was an attempt to translate the imagery of language into the vivid and lively illustrations of visual design.

The central idea is encompassed in my interpretation of common figures of speech; what I “see” when I hear these in conversation is what I’ve attempted to put to paper.

Upon embarking on this project, I reflected upon the colorful phrases and sayings I have come across.
